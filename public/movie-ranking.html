The best movies according to Erick

My Top 3 movies of all-time

ELVIS (2022)

I am a big fan of Elvis Presley, so this movie that tells his story was something that I wanted to see for a long time. The actors were great, the production was amazing, and the soundtrack was incredible.

Percy Jackson

I remember watching this movie many times when I was young. In general, I have not watched many of those famous fantasy movies, but for some reason this one was awesome for me and I watched it many times. Fun Fact: I never watched the second movie of the series.

Monsters Inc.

This cartoon from Pixar have always been one of my favorite movies of all-time. I really like the concept of the city that gets energy from the children's screams, it is very creative. What I liked the most though was the doors. You open a door and you are in a complete different location. I also enjoyed seeing all the different monsters and the roles they played inside the "fear factory".
